Earn while you learn about blockchain

First Contact Crypto is the destination platform for learning about this amazing technology.

Incorporated right into the learning experience is a reward system that pays you in cryptocurrency and teaches you how to securely transact with your newly awarded tokens.

From beginner to advanced.. First Contact Crypto provides an experience that is designed to optimize the learner's intuition and utilization.

Features

Course Content

Our course content is selected with care towards the user's experience and intuition. Many producers of the content are from well known organizations. Content is curated with great attention towards an optimized learning experience.. with a lot of fun!

Testing

Participants are incentivized to comprehend concepts by earning cryptocurrency as they learn. Our learning management system incorporates various means of testing the learner's knowledge and various means of rewarding the the user's activity.

Mechanisms are employed to incentivize the user to acquire knowledge. They are at the core of our learning managment system. Active participation by learners.. using the actual tools the user will be learning about.. and, using the currencies they've earned while learning.
